General Questions

Who can participate in IFT?

expand_more
Students from grades 9-12 across all boards (CBSE, ICSE, IB, State) are eligible to join the competition. All participants must be currently enrolled in school.

Is there any registration fee?

expand_more
No, participation in IFT is completely free. We believe in making innovation accessible to all students across India.

What are the competition phases?

expand_more
The competition has three phases: Registration & Idea Submission, Regional Rounds, and Grand Finale. Each phase has specific evaluation criteria and deadlines.
group

Team Formation

What is the team size?

expand_more
Teams can have 2-4 members. Each team must have a designated team leader who will be the primary point of contact.

Can we have team members from different schools?

expand_more
Yes, cross-school collaboration is encouraged! Teams can include members from different schools as long as all members are within the eligible grade range.

Can I change my team members later?

expand_more
Team changes are allowed only before the idea submission deadline. After submission, team composition must remain the same throughout the competition.

Submission Details

How do I submit my pitch deck?

expand_more
Log in to your student dashboard and navigate to "Idea Submission". Follow the step-by-step process to upload your pitch deck and complete the required information.

What file formats are accepted?

expand_more
We accept PDF, PPTX, and Google Slides links. Make sure your file size is under 50MB and all content is visible and properly formatted.

Can I edit my submission after uploading?

expand_more
Yes, you can edit and resubmit your idea multiple times before the submission deadline. Only the latest version will be considered for evaluation.
emoji_events

Evaluation

How is the innovation judged?

expand_more
Submissions are evaluated based on innovation, feasibility, impact, presentation quality, and team collaboration. Each criterion has specific weightage in the scoring rubric.

When will the results be announced?

expand_more
Results for each phase are announced within 2-3 weeks after the deadline. You'll receive notifications via email and on your dashboard.

What prizes can we win?

expand_more
Winners receive cash prizes, mentorship opportunities, certificates, and exposure to industry leaders. The Grand Finale winner gets exclusive incubation support for their idea.
