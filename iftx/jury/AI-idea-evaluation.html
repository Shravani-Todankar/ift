Abstract

Our project aims to provide affordable, solar-powered water filtration for rural communities. By utilizing locally sourced materials and a novel filtration membrane, we can reduce waterborne diseases by up to 40% in targeted villages. The system is modular, requiring minimal maintenance, and the solar component ensures it operates entirely off-grid.
