MediTrack — AI Health Companion

HealthTech AI/ML Wearables Mobile App
One-Line Pitch
An AI-powered health monitoring app that tracks vitals, predicts health risks, and connects students with nearby medical professionals using wearable integration and ML models trained on adolescent health data.
Problem Statement
Adolescents in India lack access to affordable, real-time health monitoring. Most health issues among students go undetected until they become critical, costing families significant medical expenses and causing preventable school absences.
Proposed Solution
MediTrack is a wearable + mobile app ecosystem. A low-cost IoT band collects vitals (heart rate, SpO2, temperature). The app runs an ML model trained on 50,000+ adolescent health records to flag anomalies. Alerts are sent to parents and the nearest empanelled doctor.
Target Market
260 million school-going students in India aged 10–18. Initial focus on Tier 2/3 cities where healthcare access is limited. TAM: ₹18,000 Cr. SAM: ₹2,400 Cr.
Business Model
Hardware: ₹499 band (subsidised via school partnerships). SaaS subscription: ₹99/month per student. B2B school licensing: ₹15,000/year for 500+ students.
