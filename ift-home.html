About IFT

Bridging the Gap for
Young Minds

Children are a powerhouse of ideas! But they lack the right platforms to express creativity, solve real problems, and showcase their potential. India's Future Tycoons (IFT) bridges this gap for ages 13-18 through mentorship, founder bootcamps, and a national pitch stage, helping students build solutions, gain innovation confidence, earn recognition, access funding, and unlock global opportunities.

About ENpower

ENpower is a future-skills ecosystem enabling young minds to build the skills, mindset, and confidence required to create meaningful real-world impact. IFT, an initiative by ENpower, provides a national platform for students to showcase their innovation and entrepreneurial potential.

Frequently Asked Questions

IFT is India's first-ever entrepreneurship search & mentorship platform for young adults (students). It allows students to showcase ideas, get expert mentorship, and refine ventures that solve real-world problems.
Students aged 13–18 years from any school in India can register and submit their ideas, either individually or as teams.
Participation in IFT is completely FREE for students.
IFT is carried out in multiple levels: Idea submission & shortlisting, interactive learning sessions, mentorship and refinement, residential boot camps, and final pitch showcase to judges.
Top ideas are invited to a 3-day residential bootcamp with 1-on-1 mentorship from experts, preparation for the grand finale pitch, and competition for prizes, recognition, and investor attention.
Cash prizes & awards, mentorship and networking opportunities, possibilities of seed funding, and exposure on national & international platforms such as NFTE and UNGCNI events.
No, it's more than a contest. IFT is an experiential learning journey that builds an entrepreneurial mindset and real-world problem-solving skills, not just idea pitching.
IFT helps participants in building life skills like creativity, critical thinking, collaboration, and leadership, and learn to convert problems into solutions.
Schools can fill in the form above & our team will get in touch with your team.
You can register or get more details on the official platform dedicated to IFT — India's Future Tycoons, via ENpower's website.
