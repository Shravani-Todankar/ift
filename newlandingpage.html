About IFT

India's Future Tycoons

Children are a powerhouse of ideas and innovation but they lack a platform to express freely, solve real-world problems, and build the confidence to innovate.

ENpower's India's Future Tycoons (IFT) bridges this gap as India's first national entrepreneurship search and mentorship platform for young innovators (13–18). We enable young minds with the skills, mindset, and courage to create real-world impact.

Children experience a journey where they learn how to identify real-world problems, build solutions, and develop entrepreneurial thinking through expert-led mentorship, founder bootcamps, and a national pitch stage.

IFT is a launchpad for confidence, creativity, and purpose, helping students transform ideas into action while gaining visibility, recognition, and access to funding and global opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is India's Future Tycoons (IFT)? add

IFT is India's first-ever entrepreneurship search & mentorship platform for young adults (students). It allows students to showcase ideas, get expert mentorship, and refine ventures that solve real-world problems.

How to bring IFT to our school? add

Schools can fill in the form on this page and our team will get in touch with your team to discuss partnership opportunities and implementation.

Who can participate in IFT? add

Students aged 13–18 years from any school in India can register and submit their ideas, either individually or as teams.

What are the fees to participate in IFT? add

Participation in IFT is completely FREE for students.

What is the structure of the program? add

IFT is carried out in multiple levels: Idea submission & shortlisting, Interactive learning sessions, Mentorship and refinement of ideas, Residential boot camps, and Final pitch showcase to judges.

What happens at the bootcamp and finale? add

Top ideas of the year are invited to a 3-day residential bootcamp where students get 1-on-1 mentorship from industry experts & entrepreneurs, prepare for the grand finale pitch, and compete for prizes, recognition, and investor attention.

What can participants win? add

Participants can win cash prizes & awards, mentorship and networking opportunities with expert judges, possibilities of seed funding for their venture ideas, and exposure on national & international platforms such as NFTE and UNGCNI events.

Is it just a competition? add

No, it's more than a contest. IFT is an experiential learning journey that builds an entrepreneurial mindset and real-world problem-solving skills, not just idea pitching.

How does IFT benefit students beyond prizes? add

IFT helps participants in building life skills like creativity, critical thinking, collaboration, and leadership, and learn to convert problems into solutions.
